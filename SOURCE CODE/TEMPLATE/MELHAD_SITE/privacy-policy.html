Privacy Policy

MelHad Investment respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, contact details (email, phone number, address), and payment details when you place an order or communicate with us. We also collect information about your interaction with our website for the purpose of improving user experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill orders.
  • To send you order updates, newsletters, and promotional offers.

How We Protect Your Information

We use secure encryption methods to protect your personal and payment information during transmission. Your information is stored securely and is only accessible by authorized personnel within our organization.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.
